I have no idols. I admire work, dedication and competence. - Ayrton Senna

Hello, my name is Jan Jagiela, but call me Johnny. I live in Starachowice – small city near Kielce in Poland. By heart I'm a humanist, but recently I got interested in programming. It has been front-end which caught my eye. So I decided to prove that Humanists Can Code.

I like to know things, and how they work. I always ask questions to expand my knowledge. It's always a good idea to leverage on someone else's experience.

While writing I'm trying to keep the code simple and easy to read. I'm still learning, and I know that only hard work can make me a better programmer. That is why I contribute to open source projects on GitHub. Whether I stick to front-end or venture deeper into server-side this experience is invaluable.

For entertainment I play video games or watch some e-sports tournaments. However that's not all. Hiking around local woods and mountains, visiting medieval events and ruins – that is what I choose on weekends.

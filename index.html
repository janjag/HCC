About Me

My photo

Hello, my name is John Jagiela. Like the title of this page suggest I'm a humanist, but recently I switched my interest to front-end. I'm years old, and I live in Starachowice – small city near Kielce in Poland. Currently unemployed.

I always liked to know how things work. To expand my knowledge about web I've asked my good friend Lukasz “op1ekun” Piatkowski to explain me the basics. And so the story begins...

I've completed some on-line courses on Code School about HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Git. While writing I'm trying to keep the code simple and easy to read. I can't say that I'm good at it – I'm still novice, but will improve by putting all my current skills to use in this and next projects.

For entertainment I play video games or watch some e-sports tournaments – yup, nothing special here. However that's not all. Hiking around local woods and mountains, visiting medieval events and ruins – that is what I choose on weekends.
